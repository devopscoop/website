Join the Cooperative

We are always looking for like-minded individuals and organizations to collaborate with!

As a Member

  • You will be added to the devopscoop organization with write access to all repositories, so you can create pull requests and start helping out!
  • You may request access to our group chat (currently Signal)
  • You may vote on issues and propose issues to vote on
  • You may propose new repositories to be added to the organization

How to Join

  1. Fork codeberg.org/devopscoop/organization
  2. Sign the bottom of governance.md
  3. Create a PR

Other Ways to Help

  • Tell people about devops.coop
  • Upvote our repos
